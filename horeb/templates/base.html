Our Services

WHAT WE DO

Ground Water Services

Ground Water Exploration and Assessment

Aquifer Characterization

We evaluate the properties of aquifers, including porosity, permeability, and transmissivity, to assess their suitability for water supply or other purposes.

Hydrogeological survey

Our experts use the right technology for groundwater assessment. Resistivity (VES, HEP, ERT) and Electromagnetic (EM) methods are the main geophysical methods applied.

Well Design and Installation

Our experts design and oversee the installation of wells, solar and structures for monitoring or extraction of groundwater.

Groundwater Quality Assessment

Water Sampling and Analysis

We collect water samples and conduct laboratory analysis to assess groundwater quality and compliance with regulatory standards.

Contaminant Assessment

Our team identifies and assesses the presence of contaminants in groundwater and provides remediation strategies when necessary.

Aquifer Testing and Modeling

Pumping Tests

To understand the aquifer properties.

Groundwater Flow Modelling

Utilizing state-of-the-art software, we develop groundwater flow models to predict groundwater movement, availability and water balance.

Well-field Design and Management

Well-field Design

We design well-fields for optimal water extraction and distribution.

Monitoring and Maintenance

Our team offers ongoing well-field monitoring and maintenance services to ensure efficient operation.

Groundwater Resource Management

Sustainable Resource Planning

We assist in developing sustainable groundwater management plans, including recharge strategies and withdrawal limits.

Regulatory Compliance

Our experts help clients navigate regulatory requirements related to groundwater use and protection.
